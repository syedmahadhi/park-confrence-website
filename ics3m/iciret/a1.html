PARK COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY
 

The Park Trust promoted by Dr.P.V.Ravi, Ph.D., the pioneer of the concept of self financed Technical Education in Tamilnadu, is serving more than 38 years in the field of Technical Education.Park college of Engineering and Technology(PCET) started in the year 1997 is located in Coimbatore has a serene atmosphere conductive for learning.It is Approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University,Accredited by NAAC and NBA and accorded with ISO certification.The institution has established a position of its own by upgrading continuously with the agile needs of the industrial world.

 

The Trust with nearly 20 education institutions is dedicated in fulfilling its responsibilities to the socity in molding the students to be good citizens of the nation.
