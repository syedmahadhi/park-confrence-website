About iCS3M

Sharing new ideas among the experts in the field and updating the existing ones are much required to acquire excellence in the sector of Smart Materials, Mechanisms and Manufacturing to have its influence in the technical sustainability for the recent development.

This conference aims in giving a platform for the academicians, researchers and technocrats to share and acquire knowledge in the field of Smart materials, Mechanisms and Manufacturing which comprises but not limited to Piezoelectric materials, Photovoltaic materials, Magnetostrictive materials, Temperature responsive polymers, Halochromic materials, Chromogenic systems, Photomechanical materials, Self healing materials, Dielectric elastomers, Thermoelectric materials, All broad inclusions of Mechanical centric mechanisms and All divisions of Manufacturing and Production Engineering.

This conference also aims in removing the barriers in the development of smart materials, Mechanisms and Manufacturing through outcome based discussions among the academicians, researchers and technocrats. Huge chances are in front of the delegates of the conference to achieve international recognition, since many reputed Journals have given their willingness to publish the presented papers after basic Peer review.
